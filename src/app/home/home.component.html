See like never before

No more scrolling through long and cryptic text files or seeing only one item at a time. With OWLGrEd you can see your whole ontology at a glance.

Edit like a Pro

OWLGrEd is easy to understand and use even for "non-ontologists". You can give it without hesitation to your industrial partners.

Adapt for your needs

You can choose which OWL entity types are included in the visualization. You can change the automatic layout of the ontology as well as its graphical style.

Share your insight

After you have visualized and fine-tuned your ontology, you can export it as an image for use in documentation or inside a web page.
